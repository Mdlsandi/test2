About Me

The ‘Code like a Girl’ programme aims to develop coding skills and valuable life skills for girls aged 14 to 18, and encourages them to consider the uptake of ICT and STEM subjects. In addition, it provides influential mentors for the girls to inspire them to be passionate about technology and its possibilities.

My Career Journey

I matriculated at Tetlanyo Secondary School in 2013. Then in 2015, I found out about a new course called Data Science, I was immediately interested and apply for the course at Sol Plaatje University which is based in Kimberley. I got accepted for the 3 year course, I was so happy because it required learners who studied Maths and Science in high school. In 2017, I completed the course and graduated in record time. I then looked for a job to get some practical experience to enhance my knowledge of theoretical concepts I learned in varsity, I got an internship at Department of Education. Currently, I am pursuing my Honours in Data Science and also working as an intern at Vodacom. So far, it's been a exciting journey without of learning opportunities.
